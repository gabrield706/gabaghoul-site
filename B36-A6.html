ghouligan is a fusion of goth, metal, and electronic influences with a Latin kick.
A producer songwriter with Latin heritage and spooky sensibilities, ghouligan’s music goes dark, goes light, and has audiences moving in trances throughout.
Unlike the aggressiveness of metal and the cold, brooding vibes of dark post-punk, ghouligan embodies a struggle between pleasure and discontent, as he’s constantly
working to keep a Latin groove alive amidst a chaos of noise and distortion.

Subject: Latin Goth Metal Act Seeking LA Venue

To Whom It May Concern

My name is Gabriel Diaz, and I’m the producer behind ghouligan, an alternative rock act blending Metal, Goth, Electronic, and Latin influences.
Since debuting four months ago, I’ve performed at multiple venues in Bakersfield, including the historic Fox Theater and the Padre Hotel,
drawing crowds of 40-60 people with tickets priced at $15-20. With 4,700 Instagram followers, 2,000 TikTok followers, and over 1.3 million
streams across major platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Youtube Music, Tidal), ghouligan has been praised by local critics as “a promising new act
reviving alternative rock for a younger generation.” While I’ve built momentum in my hometown, I’m now looking to bring my first performance to Los Angeles.
I’m interested in performing at your venue and would love to discuss the possibility of hosting a show.
Feel free to check out my music at www.ghouliganmusic.com and reach out if you’re interested.

Best,
Gabriel Diaz
