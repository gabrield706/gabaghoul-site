Diatonic/Direct Song

"Grass":

VERSE:
I'm looking at a bunch of grass
It's very green
But is it even greener on the other side?

On the other side

Isn't it nice
To finally touch some?
But when I'm there
I'm still trapped inside my head

On the other side

CHORUS:
And I go outside
Because I’m loosing my mind
And the voices won’t stop
Something’s crawling in my head

Now I’m under the light
And the air feels fine
And it’s all okay
Because I’m finally on the other side

On the other side

The lyrics for this song came from looking at grass outside my window. I started playing around with grass-related sayings (ex. "grass is always greener", "touch grass"), and eventually I got to a point where the song became about someone on the verge of madness until they go outside and touch grass. This is what made my chorus go into a creepy vibe, sice I figure I could've expanded on that idea there. I made the song in the key of E and used I-V for my verse, and iii-IV for my chorus.
