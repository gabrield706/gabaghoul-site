Assignment 2 - EQ Ear Training

I thought the assignment was a lot of fun. It was so much easier to identify the filter
with the pink noise since the brightness and darkness of it all was a lot easier to notice.
My thought process with it all was to first pick up on whether the sound was more quiet or loud first,
since this gave me some direction of where the filter loudness was supposed to go.
At least with boost, the louder frequencies were easier to pinpoint but in cut, choosing a rock band sound sample didn't help much because with all of the instrument
frequencies being all over the place. I used one of my synth recordings for boost/cut
mode and I think identifying the filter got a bit better, since the sound was a lot more uniform.
While I didn't feel much of an issue switching between filter modes, it definitely became more
challenging when I got to work with audio where the fequencies varied a lot more.
