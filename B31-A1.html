Influence

1. In "Fall" by Ozean, I really like the feel that the drummer goes for in this part. It almost feels like horses galloping but it's also subtle enough to where the cymbals sound like 16th notes and not at the same time. It really adds to the track's dreamy texture. 2. In "In Your House" by The Cure, I really like how Robert Smith lets his guitar strings ring on for the song's main melody. His guitar line is so simple but those open strings really add to the song's borderline-ominous texture. 3. In "El Condor Pasa" by Los Incas, the panpipes really remind me of my dad's stories about visiting his family in the Peruvian mountain ranges. Every time I hear panpipes I get this mental image of being high up in the Andes, which is why I tried getting a panpipe sound by using a virtual synth.
